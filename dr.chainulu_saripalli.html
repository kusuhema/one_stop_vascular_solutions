Dr Chainulu Saripalli

MBBS DNB Radio Diagnosis
Regd. No. 75892
Consultant Interventional Radiology
Specialist in Peripheral Vascular Disease and Minimally Invasive Onco Interventions

Dr. Chainulu Saripalli is a distinguished Vascular and Interventional Radiologist with a decade of experience, contributing significantly to the field of medical care. His academic journey commenced with an MBBS from Rangaraya Medical College in Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh. Driven by a passion for Radiology, he pursued post-graduate training at Apollo Hospitals in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, specializing in this dynamic field.

Elevating his expertise further, Dr. Saripalli underwent specialized training in Interventional Radiology at CARE Hospitals in Hyderabad. Notably, he transitioned into a faculty role at the institute, where he played a pivotal role in various post-graduate training programs, showcasing his commitment to education and mentorship.

Dr. Chainulu Saripalli's particular interest lies in peripheral arterial disease and dialysis access interventions. His dedication to advancing medical knowledge is evident through his presentations of papers and posters at numerous state and national conferences. Additionally, he has contributed to the literature with published articles, demonstrating a commitment to sharing insights and advancements in his field.

His exemplary work was recognized on the international stage when he was honored with the Young Achiever Award at the Asian Oceanian Congress of Radiology (AOCR) in Japan on September 24 - 28, 2014. The award specifically acknowledged his innovative approach to evaluating acute abdomen in oncology patients.

Dr. Saripalli specializes in peripheral vascular, dialysis access, and onco-interventions, aligning with his memberships in prestigious organizations such as the Indian Society for Vascular and Interventional Radiology, the Indian Medical Association, and the Indian Society for Radiology and Imaging. His dual focus on clinical excellence and contribution to medical literature solidifies his standing as a distinguished figure in the realm of Vascular and Interventional Radiology.
