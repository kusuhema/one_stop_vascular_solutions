Dr Pritee Sharma

DNB (Gen.Surg), DNB (Vasc.Surg)
Regd. No. 07859
Consultant Vascular & Endovascular surgeon
Specialist in Diabetic foot, chronic wound care specialist and Aesthetic Phlebology

Dr. Pritee Sharma stands as a distinguished Vascular & Endovascular Surgeon in Banjara Hills, amassing over 19 years of experience and demonstrating expertise in every facet of her field. Her academic journey is marked by notable achievements, beginning with an MBBS from Topiwala National Medical College and B.Y.L Nair Hospital in 2005. Dr. Sharma furthered her qualifications with D.N.B. in General Surgery at Jagjivan Ram Hospital, Western Railway, Mumbai Central, from 2006-09, and DNB in Peripheral Vascular Surgery at Army Hospital Research & Referral, Delhi University, New Delhi, in 2013.She made her mentors and alma mater proud by bagging GOLD MEDAL in DNB Peripheral Vascular surgery.

Dr Sharma is recognized for her expertise in Varicose veins, Deep vein thrombosis, Pelvic vein disease, Limb bypass surgeries (open & Endovascular), Carotid artery stenosis, Cancer related surgeries and Vascular access procedures for Chronic Kidney disease patients.

Due to her dire desire of updating her knowledge she has learnt skills from the world renowned masters in the field of Diabetic foot and wound care specialists. She specializes in providing prophylactic and therapeutic surgical procedures in Diabetic foot patients and is an expert in chronic leg wounds management.

Dr Sharma is a member of Vascular Society of India, Association of Surgeons of India, Diabetic foot society of India and American Limb preservation society. She is on the Editorial board for reputed journals like Indian Journal of Vascular Surgery and Journal of Vascular surgery –Vascular Insights. She is Diplomat of National board teaching faculty for Peripheral Vascular Surgery and conducts super specialty teaching programs for National board. She is Executive committee member for Venous association of India. She has 14 National and International publications in Index journals and has authored two chapters in textbooks of Vascular Surgery. Being a renowned faculty she has been invited to deliver talks at International vascular surgery conferences and has delivered till now 30 talks at various National & International Vascular surgery conferences. Apart from her academic accolades she has been pioneer in spreading vascular disease awareness in Telangana and Maharashtra by regular interactions with Anganwadi workers , National integrated Medical association and Association of Unani Medicine.

Dr. Pritee Sharma's commitment to advancing medical knowledge, spreading awareness about vascular diseases to common man and providing exceptional patient care solidifies her reputation as a leading figure in the realm of vascular surgery.
